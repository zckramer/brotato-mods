[h1]DamageMeter - Real-Time Damage Tracking[/h1]

Track your damage in real-time! DamageMeter displays comprehensive damage statistics during runs, showing total damage dealt and your top damage sources. Perfect for analyzing builds and optimizing strategies.

[h2]Features[/h2]

[list]
[*][b]Total Damage Display[/b] - Progress bars showing each player's damage with percentages
[*][b]Top Damage Sources[/b] - See your best weapons, items, and abilities ranked by damage
[*][b]Item Grouping[/b] - Automatically groups identical items (e.g., multiple turrets)
[*][b]Smart Tracking[/b] - Tracks weapons, items, spawned entities (turrets, landmines), and abilities
[*][b]Visual Indicators[/b] - Rarity colors, cursed markers, item icons with rounded backgrounds
[*][b]Performance Optimized[/b] - Intelligent caching for smooth gameplay
[*][b]Fully Configurable[/b] - Customize opacity, number of sources, DPS display, and more
[/list]

[h2]What Gets Tracked?[/h2]

[list]
[*]All weapons and their damage output
[*]Damage-dealing items
[*]Spawned entities (Engineering turrets, Screwdriver landmines, Pocket Factory turrets)
[*]Character abilities
[/list]

[h2]Configuration[/h2]

[b]With ModOptions (Optional):[/b]

If you have ModOptions installed, configure all settings in-game via [b]Options â†’ Mods â†’ DamageMeter[/b]:
[list]
[*][b]Opacity[/b] - Adjust UI transparency (0.3-1.0, default: 1.0)
[*][b]Number of Sources[/b] - Show 1-25 top damage sources (default: 6)
[*][b]Show Item Count[/b] - Display count for grouped items (default: on)
[*][b]Show DPS[/b] - Display damage per second (default: off)
[*][b]Show Percentage[/b] - Show percentage values relative to top player (default: on)
[*][b]Hide Damage Bar (Solo)[/b] - Hide total damage bar when playing solo (default: off)
[/list]

All changes apply instantly without restarting!

[b]Without ModOptions:[/b]

The mod works perfectly with default settings (shown above). No configuration needed!

[h2]Optional Enhancement[/h2]

[b]ModOptions[/b] (by Oudstand) - Adds in-game configuration UI for customizing settings. The mod works great without it using sensible defaults!
